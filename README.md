Project Description
FoodShare is a web-based platform that connects restaurants, donors, and event organizers with NGOs and volunteers to efficiently manage surplus food distribution. The system ensures that edible food reaches those in need before it expires, reducing food waste and fighting hunger in communities.
The platform provides real-time notifications, interactive maps using Leaflet.js, priority-based food sorting based on freshness, and comprehensive order tracking from posting to delivery.

Problem Statement
Every year, millions of tons of edible food go to waste while millions of people go hungry. The main challenges are:
Lack of Communication: Restaurants and donors don't know who needs food
Time Sensitivity: Food expires quickly and needs urgent distribution
Coordination Issues: No system to track who accepts, delivers, or rejects food
Manual Processes: Existing systems rely on phone calls and WhatsApp groups
No Priority System: No way to prioritize food that expires soonest
